About Us

NIMTA is a vocational institute where individuals are trained on various courses and trades; such as web designing, python programming, fashion designing and lots more. It is being organized by the Nigerian Officer's Wives Association NAOWA, headed by the NAOWA president Mrs Salamatu F Yahaya, which is under the care and the responsibility of the rector, who is the caretaker of the facilities and students in her custody, and also responsible for the discipline of students because surely indiscipline can't be condoned in NIMTA. The institute is located at Mambilla Barracks, Asokoro, Abuja.

NIMTA also offers admission into an National Institute of Design(NID) program which is equivalent to Ordinary National Diploma(OND), and Simultaneous Multithreading(SMT) course which includes computing science and programming

With the motive to improve the state of the barrack youths, NAOWA organised a training programme to enable the barrack youth to be equipped with computer literacy and other trade; Such as Web Design, Python Programming, Get Connected, IT Essentials, Cyber Security and Fashion Designing.
