HISTORY OF NIMTA(NAOWA INSTITUTE OF MANAGEMENT AND TECHNOLOGY ABUJA)

THE NAOWA INSTITUTE OF MANAGEMENT AND TECHNOLOGY ABUJA(NIMTA)

It was not an accredited institution initially.The Project implementation team made a big impact on the development of naowa VTC ,they also aided in the foundation of the institute to provide skills acquisition to the military wives to enable them to be more productive and innovative.The implementation team include:Mrs Fatima Haijju Abdulkadir,Mrs Hajara Pyeng, Mrs Rose Chong,Mrs Christiana Bassey,Mrs Emem Isang,Mrs Salamatu Ndagi,Mrs Hadizah Sani Umar etc.Project Execution was done by Boushi Nigeria Limited.

In the year 2021 when ASUU(Academic staffs union of university)declared eight months strike towards federals and states university in Nigeria which affected barracks youth both academically and mentally.Mrs Salamatu F Yahaya made a partnership with CISCO Networking Academy,she hold a six weeks intensive programs for youth in the barracks classified in two parts which are undergraduate and ssce holders. The programs was meant to implement and educate youth through various means of acquiring Networking knowledge.The programs was divided into five deparments namely: IT Essential,Get Connected,Web Design,Cyber security and Phyton.The goal to be achieved during this programs was to encourage barracks youth to acquire a skill and to get into a new age of technology and how to use the skills towards their advantage and also benefit from it. Mrs salamatu F yahaya has proven herself to be one of the best naowo president to go down in history.Beside being the naowa president of the institute,she has contributed towards the Nigeria armed forces and the Federal Republic of Nigeria.
